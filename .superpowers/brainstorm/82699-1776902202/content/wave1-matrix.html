Live Tutor Canvas Wave 1

Balanced primitives first. Diagram-heavy + reasoning-heavy. Click any cards that feel strongest or most off.

Recommendation
Wave 1: core live-tutor workspaces

Image Hotspot

Tap exact regions on a diagram, map, circuit, body part, or labeled figure.

Subjects: biology, geography, anatomy, physics, geometry

Lively: glow target, tutor highlight pulse, near-miss feedback, reveal labels after submit

Timeline Builder

Drag events, stages, or milestones onto a chronological or ordered axis.

Subjects: history, literature, evolution, scientific procedures, civics

Lively: snap-to-era lanes, before/after hints, tutor scrubbing through sequence

Continuous Axis

Place a value, estimate, or example along a line or 2-point spectrum.

Subjects: physics, statistics, geography climate bands, tone, confidence, economics

Lively: live formula preview, shaded ranges, tutor nudges toward precision

Venn Diagram

Drag ideas or examples into shared and non-shared set regions.

Subjects: logic, compare/contrast, classification, grammar, civics

Lively: overlap glow, partial-credit grouping, tutor probes on why something belongs in the middle

Token Builder

Assemble expressions, equations, statements, or structured phrases from pieces.

Subjects: algebra, chemistry equations, grammar, logic, code reasoning

Lively: slots magnetize, syntax-validity hints, tutor reads the construction aloud

Process Flow

Build a chain, cycle, or branch of causes, steps, and outputs.

Subjects: biology, chemistry, history causality, writing structure, economics

Lively: arrows animate on submit, tutor traces the path, branch mistakes become visible
